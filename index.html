Introducing the HomeScope 18Fr Catheter

A transparent, flexible, medical-grade cystoscope engineered to capture panoramic views of the bladder and bladder neck. Floating in a holographic stage, HomeScope showcases its multi-camera distal tip, atraumatic rounded head, and seamless body that glides comfortably while returning diagnostic-grade imagery.

  • Rotating holographic projection spotlights the full-length catheter and smooth, clear silicone body.
  • Embedded panoramic cameras glow with soft halos, highlighting wide-angle visualization.
  • Rim lighting and subtle reflections emphasize the premium materials and manufacturing quality.

Why it matters

The Problem We're Solving

Bladder cancer has one of the highest recurrence rates of any cancer, requiring frequent monitoring. Current cystoscopy methods are costly, inconvenient, and often delayed.

Limited Access to Care

63% of US counties lack a urologist. Rural patients travel 34 miles on average to the nearest specialist.

High Societal Cost

Bladder cancer is one of the most expensive diagnoses to treat due to repeat procedures and lost time from work.

High Recurrence Rates

Non-muscle invasive cases require relentless surveillance cystoscopies with little relief for patients.

Lack of Follow-Up

Scheduling constraints and access barriers lead to missed appointments, increasing progression risk.

The Surveillance Challenge

Cystoscopy remains the gold standard for bladder cancer monitoring, yet the intensity of follow-ups and lack of portable tools create overwhelming barriers. HomeScope redefines the encounter with guided workflows and asynchronous review.

Solution

A Medical Device Built for Anywhere

A novel at-home cystoscopy device that combines medical-grade precision with patient-friendly design, making regular bladder monitoring accessible without sacrificing diagnostic fidelity.

The Technology

An 18Fr catheter with panoramic optics, onboard capture, and post-processing that compiles stills and video for urologists. Biocompatible materials, sterile packaging, and reliable lighting ensure repeatable procedures anywhere.
